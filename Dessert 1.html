    • Mug Brownies

    Serves 1. 550 calories per serving, 14g of fat

    Ingredients:

    • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
    • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    • 1 ½ tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
    • Pinch of salt
    • 2 tablespoons chocolate chips, plus more for topping
    • 1 ½ tablespoons vegetable oil, melted coconut oil, or melted butter
    • 3 tablespoons milk
    • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Ice cream, whipped cream, powdered sugar, berries for garnish

    Instructions:

    1. Place sugar, flour, cocoa powder, and salt in a mug.
    2. Use a small whisk or fork, and stir until combined and free of lumps.
    3. Stir in chocolate chips.
    4. Add oil, milk, and vanilla; stir with a spoon or small rubber spatula until just combined (try not to overmix it).
    5. Sprinkle a few additional chocolate chips over the top.
    6. Cook in the microwave for about 90 seconds (microwave times may vary).
    7. Eat immediately with ice cream, whipped cream, powdered sugar, or berries.
