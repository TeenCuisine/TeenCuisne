French Toast

5 min prep time / 5 min cook time
Serves two, 190 calories per serving

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices hearty sandwich bread

Instructions:

  1. Whisk egg, vanilla, and cinnamon in a shallow dish. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread slices in the egg mixture, turning to coat evenly on both sides.
  3. Cook bread slices on a lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until cooked through and browned on both sides. Serve with Easy Spiced Syrup (recipe follows), if desired. Voila, easy French toast.
